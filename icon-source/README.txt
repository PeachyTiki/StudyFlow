DROP YOUR ICON FILE IN HERE
===========================

Put your icon image in this folder, then tell Claude "icon is in".

Accepted formats:  PNG, JPG, SVG
Recommended size:  512x512 px or larger (square works best)

Claude will then:
  • Resize it to 192x192 and 512x512 PNG
  • Replace icon.svg / icon-192.png / icon-512.png
  • Rebuild the zip in apk/StudyFlow.zip
